# Animal Exposure Disposition Engine Evidence Synthesis

## Scope, evidence base, and evidence-strength rubric

This synthesis is decision-focused: it translates high-authority guidance into algorithm-ready nodes, risk strata, and disposition thresholds for a public-facing + clinician-usable triage/disposition engine covering mammalian exposures (bites, scratches, saliva contact, and indirect contact). It prioritizes hazards that materially change disposition—especially rabies/lyssaviruses, serious bite-wound bacterial infection risks (including *Pasteurella* and *Capnocytophaga*), cat-scratch disease, and tetanus. citeturn8view4turn7view1turn15view0turn7view0turn18view0turn8view0turn14view0
