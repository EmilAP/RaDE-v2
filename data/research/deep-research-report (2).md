Mosquito‑borne disposition logic must explicitly separate:
- **Plausibility of transmission** (mosquito exposure + geography + season/outbreak context) from **probability of disease** (often low per individual bite but meaningfully altered by geography/outbreak activity). citeturn5search1turn5search24turn9view0  
- **Severity if missed** (e.g., *Plasmodium falciparum* malaria can progress to severe illness and death without prompt treatment; multiple arboviruses can cause neuroinvasive disease requiring hospitalization). citeturn7search4turn2search4turn2search2  
- **Time‑sensitive intervention** (e.g., malaria requires urgent testing/treatment; dengue requires close monitoring in the critical phase; rashes/arthralgias without warning signs are often outpatient). citeturn0search4turn0search2turn6search5
