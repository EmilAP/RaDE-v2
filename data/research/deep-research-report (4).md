# Vector-Borne Disease Disposition Engine Evidence Synthesis

## Scope, evidence approach, and disposition goals

This synthesis supports a safe, public-facing and clinician-usable vector-borne triage/disposition engine for the RaDE platform. It is designed to map (a) exposure-only scenarios, (b) symptom-based triage, and (c) travel-returned illness triage into standardized dispositions: no care needed, self-care/monitoring, routine medical assessment, urgent assessment, emergency care.
